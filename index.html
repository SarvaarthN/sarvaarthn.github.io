GantryMate-200 +

C ‚Ä¢ ESP-IDF ‚Ä¢ Onshape
3-axis automated gantry system for high-precision screwing...

Designed and implemented a 3-axis automated gantry system for high-precision screwing and unscrewing of approximately 280 miniature (1 mm) screws on sensitive LED panels.

Developed ESP32-based firmware using ESP-IDF to enable coordinated multi-axis motion, homing routines, and precise positioning control.

Integrated NEMA 17 stepper motors driven by DRV8825 and TMC2208 stepper drivers with microstepping, along with geared DC motors for toolhead actuation to achieve controlled torque.

Designed and fabricated a custom PCB for motor driver integration, power distribution, and control signal routing.

Achieved sub-millimeter positioning accuracy (0.05 mm) with strong emphasis on repeatability and safe operation near delicate circuitry.
